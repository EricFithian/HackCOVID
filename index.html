FAQs

Where?

HackCOVID will be an international virtual hackathon!

When?

HackCOVID starts 3/21 (Saturday) at 0800 AM PDT and ends on 3/22 (Sunday) at 1100 AM PDT.

Who can participate?

Everyone!

I want to volunteer or mentor! How can I help?

Send an email to team@hackcovid.org, and we'll get back to you!

How can I become a sponsor?

Email us at team@hackcovid.org with "Sponsorship" in your subject line!

How many people can we have on each team?

Any number you want up to five. You can even work on multiple teams and submit as many projects as you want!

What can I make?

We welcome anything you have in mind that will help defeat coronavirus. When the event starts, we will also provide several ideas and prompts to help you get started.
